Gravure Printing Cylinders, 2016-Present

I now work at American Yuncheng Gravure Cylinder, Inc. in Spartanburg, South Carolina, manufacturing gravure printing cylinders. Yuncheng, headquartered in Shanxi, China, is the largest manufacturer of gravure cylinders in the world.

My responsibilities include prepress, customer service, and translation. Due to being bilingual, I sooner or later get involved in most other things that happen in the building.

An upcoming responsibility is to build a new website for the company. I believe DigitalCrafts will be able to help me with that.
